Model Introduction

We have implemented a number of fake news detection algorithm models, including content-based fake news detection algorithms, social background based fake news detection algorithms, and knowledge perception based fake news detection algorithms.

Content based

The content-based fake news detection algorithm utilizes textual and visual information in news content to detect fake news.

  • ENDEF
  • M3FEND
  • MDFEND
  • TEXTCNN
  • CAFE
  • EANN
  • HMCAN
  • MCAN
  • MFAN
  • SAFE
  • SpotFake

Social context

The fake news detection algorithm based on social background detects and distinguishes fake news by investigating social background information related to news articles, especially through dissemination on social media.

  • BIGCN
  • DUDEF
  • EBGCN
  • EDDFN
  • FANG
  • GCNFN
